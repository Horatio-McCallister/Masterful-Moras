A book chronicling the life of Horatio McCallister written by none other then the Miqo'te himself. The book covers the humble beginnings of his life and the struggles of coming from a family of immense wealth. Having a family of what he considers deadbeats, he took the inheritance he recived from his parents and started a company doing the one thing he loved...fishing and being alone. The seed money was enough to fuel the meteoric rise of the Masterful Moras Fishing Company along with Rhel Zenator. Read on to find out about the interesting life of Horatio McCallister.
